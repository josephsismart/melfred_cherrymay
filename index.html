Our Story

What started as a simple hello grew into a love story written by God's hand. Through the years, Melfred and Cherry May held on to each other through every season — through laughter and tears, through distance and closeness, through ordinary days that became extraordinary because they faced them together.


Love is not just found in life's happiest moments, but in the quiet strength of choosing each other every single day. And now, after years of faith, patience, and countless blessings, they stand together — ready to say "I do" and begin forever.

FAQ

What time should I arrive?
Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the ceremony (1:30 PM) to allow time for seating. The ceremony begins promptly at 2:00 PM.

We are keeping our celebration intimate. If a plus one was included in your invite, their name will be specified. We appreciate your understanding.
What should I wear?
Formal attire encouraged! Wear our color palette — champagne, mocha, ivory, and dark formal tones — for cohesive and beautiful photos.
Where is the reception?
Reception at JZAR Function Hall, Gante II, Manajeros Street, Pioneer Avenue, Tagum City. Guests will be guided from the church after photo session.
