Our Story

Our story began with a simple friendship. We were just two people who shared laughter, hopes, dreams, and even life's challenges together.


Without realizing it, those ordinary moments became our most treasured memories. The person who was once simply a friend became the one we turned to in every season of life.


In each other, we found space where our hearts felt safe. We didn't simply fall in love — we fell in love with the way we could be completely ourselves.


Today, we are grateful not only for the love that brought us here, but also for the journey that shaped it. Every step, every shared experience, and every shared moment has led us into something deeper.


From friendship came trust.
From trust came love.
And from love comes a lifetime of beautiful moments yet to unfold.

"So they are no longer two but one flesh. What therefore God has joined together, let not man separate." — Matthew 19:6
