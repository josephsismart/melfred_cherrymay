Our Story

What started as a simple hello grew into a love story written by God's hand. Through the years, Fred and May held on to each other through every season — through laughter and tears, through distance and closeness, through ordinary days that became extraordinary because they faced them together.


Love is not just found in life's happiest moments, but in the quiet strength of choosing each other every single day. And now, after years of faith, patience, and countless blessings, they stand together — ready to say "I do" and begin forever. 🥹

FAQs

What is the dress code?

Smart casual or formal attire is preferred. Kindly avoid white as it is reserved for the bride. We'd love to see you in our theme color — shades of green! 💚

Is the reception seat assigned?

Yes! Our team will guide you to your table upon arrival at The Pavilion at the Watergate.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Please let us know in advance when you RSVP if you plan to bring a guest so we can make the proper arrangements.

Is there parking available?

Yes, parking is available at the venue. Guests arriving at the church may also park near the Birhen sa Kasilak Shrine.

When is the RSVP deadline?
